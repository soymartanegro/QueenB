GET TO KNOW US A BIT BETTER

In 2015 Jack and Sarah took a hard look at their city life and it was abundantly clear they weren't living the life they wanted. So they changed it.

They moved to the Borders, where Sarah grew up. An area where you aren't anonymous, you run into your high school teacher at the grocery store, you see your neighbor at the bank, and where someone always knows your name.

Queen Bee cafe is a combination of both their passions, Jack's kitchen and Sarah's bees.

Since they open, they tried to educate about how important are the bees to the enviroment. People need to understand that without bees, many of the flours, trees or even fruits would survive.

FUN FACTS ABOUT US

In the UK, many species of bee are declining, with two bumblebee species already known to be extinct.
We can all do our bit to help bees whether that’s in our gardens, balconies or windowsills. You can also chat to friends and family about how cool bees are and help them to make their wild spaces bee-friendly.
Bees do not sleep – but they do remain motionless to preserve vital energy for the next day of foraging.
A hive will produce on average 11kg of honey during a season.
If you find a bumblebee which appears to be struggling, it may be that it is just resting, particularly if the bee is a queen in early spring. If you think the bee is struggling the best thing to do is gently put the bee onto a bee-friendly flower.
Bees are able to extract heavy metals and pollutants when producing honey, so they can actually create very clean honey in our cities.
